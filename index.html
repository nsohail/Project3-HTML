ABOUT THE COURSE

Check out what students from the course have done

The design and usability of a site are both quality attributes that assess how efficient, yet pleasing to the eye, a site is. At George Mason University we are proud to have several courses that help to improve student’s methods in achieving a great, usable design.

AVT 415 introduces students to the creative process of responsive web design and concepts of user-centered design by creating websites for mobile, tablet and desktop. Class of Fall 2014 consisted of students with a variety of distinct design styles. Student projects ranged from mobile site design to desktop site re-design.
